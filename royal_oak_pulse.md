# Pub Pulse — The Royal Oak (Wolverhampton)

## Executive Snapshot
Overall, The Royal Oak enjoys a strong reputation with an average rating of 4.64 from 206 reviews. Guests appreciate the friendly atmosphere and welcoming staff, but there are concerns about bar staff behaviour during busy times. Sentiment counts show 189 positive, 14 neutral, and 3 negative reviews.

## What Guests Love
- **Staff Warmth**: 52% positive. “Great friendly staff, a very welcoming atmosphere.” — Stacey Jagpal
- **Community Feel**: 11% positive. “Great family and community pub with the best beer garden.” — Ewan Litton
- **Cleanliness & Environment**: 43% positive. “Lovely garden, pub very clean and decorated nicely.” — Stacey Jagpal
- **Food Quality**: 4% positive. Guests enjoy the offerings, though some feedback indicates room for improvement.
- **Value**: 7% positive. Many feel they get good value for their experience.

## What Hurts
- **Bar Staff Behaviour**: A recurring issue with staff shouting and swearing during busy times. “Bar staff shouting at us to form a queue…” — Tony Davies
- **Cleanliness Concerns**: Some guests noted issues with littering and cleanliness in outdoor areas.
- **Food Execution**: A few comments suggest food quality could be improved.

## Trends & Direction of Travel
The last 90 days show a strong average rating of 5.0 from 5 reviews, which is an improvement over the all-time average of 4.64 from 206 reviews.
